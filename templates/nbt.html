💡 Top NBT Study Tips

1

Start Early

Begin at least 3 months before your test date. Skills-based tests cannot be crammed.

2

Read Every Day

Academic Literacy improves through reading — news articles, editorials and academic texts help most.

3

Revise Grade 12 Maths

The MAT test is curriculum-based. Strong Grade 12 maths revision is your best MAT strategy.

4

Practise with Data

For QL, practise reading graphs and tables. Work with real-life percentages and financial scenarios.

5

Time Yourself

Practise under timed conditions — the NBT is a long sitting and time management is key.

6

Register Early

Venues fill up! Register at nbt.ac.za as soon as registration opens each year.
